Plain-language summary

Summary

MobileGroup, Inc. ("MobileGroup," "MGI," "we," "us," or "our") respects privacy and aims to handle personal data responsibly. We collect personal data only when needed to operate our website, respond to inquiries, provide and support our software services, enable ticketing or point-of-sale workflows, improve security, comply with legal obligations, and communicate with customers and partners.

01

We collect data for clear purposes.

Examples include contact details, account or transaction records, support messages, device data, and data needed by ticketing or POS services.

02

We share data only when appropriate.

We may share information with clients, service providers, payment or hosting partners, and authorities when legally required.

03

You can request privacy support.

You may contact us to ask about access, correction, objection, deletion or blocking, portability, and other rights available under applicable law.

Where this applies

Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data processed by MobileGroup, Inc. in connection with:

  • our corporate website and online contact forms;
  • software consultancy, customization, ecommerce integration, ticketing, point-of-sale, loyalty, support, and related services;
  • communications with clients, partners, vendors, applicants, and other business contacts; and
  • systems where MobileGroup determines the purpose and means of processing personal data.

For some software or ticketing implementations, MobileGroup may process personal data on behalf of a client, cinema, venue, merchant, organizer, or other partner. In those cases, that organization may be the personal information controller, and its own privacy notice may also apply.

Collection sources

How we collect data

We may collect personal data directly from you, from the organization you represent, from clients or partners that use our solutions, from payment, hosting, analytics, communications, or support providers, and from systems or devices that generate operational logs. We may also receive information from public or business sources when needed for legitimate business purposes.

Purpose and lawful basis

How we use data

We use personal data for purposes that are relevant, legitimate, and proportionate, including to:

  • respond to inquiries and provide requested information;
  • create, configure, operate, maintain, customize, and support software solutions;
  • process reservations, ticketing, point-of-sale, ecommerce, loyalty, or related service workflows;
  • authenticate users, manage access rights, prevent misuse, and protect systems;
  • deliver customer support, troubleshooting, updates, and service communications;
  • administer contracts, invoices, procurement, audits, and business operations;
  • improve products, services, system reliability, user experience, and quality assurance;
  • send marketing or event communications when permitted by law or consented to by the recipient;
  • evaluate job applications and manage recruitment; and
  • comply with legal, regulatory, tax, accounting, reporting, dispute resolution, and security requirements.

Depending on the context, our lawful basis may include consent, contract performance, compliance with legal obligations, legitimate interests, protection of vital interests, or another basis allowed by applicable data protection laws.

Disclosures

How we share data

We do not sell personal data. We may share personal data with:

  • Clients, venues, merchants, organizers, or operators where needed to deliver ticketing, POS, ecommerce, support, reporting, or related services.
  • Service providers and processors such as hosting, cloud infrastructure, communications, payment gateway, cybersecurity, analytics, professional services, and customer support providers.
  • Business partners and integration providers where needed for approved integrations or client-directed workflows.
  • Regulators, courts, government agencies, law enforcement, or auditors where required or permitted by law.
  • Successors or transaction parties in connection with a merger, restructuring, acquisition, financing, or transfer of business assets, subject to appropriate protections.

When we use third-party service providers, we expect them to process personal data only for authorized purposes and to apply appropriate confidentiality, privacy, and security safeguards.

Transfers

Cross-border processing

Some providers, infrastructure, support resources, or systems may be located outside the Philippines. When personal data is transferred or accessed internationally, we use reasonable safeguards designed to protect personal data in accordance with applicable law and contractual requirements.

Protection

Retention and security

Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law, contract, audit, tax, accounting, dispute resolution, security, or legitimate business requirements. When personal data is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete, anonymize, archive, or securely dispose of it.

Security

We use reasonable organizational, physical, and technical safeguards designed to protect personal data against accidental, unlawful, or unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, loss, misuse, or destruction. No website, software, transmission method, or storage system is completely secure, so we encourage users to use strong passwords, protect devices, and report suspected security issues promptly.

Choices and rights

Your privacy rights

Subject to applicable law and verification of your identity, you may have rights to:

  • Be informed about how your personal data is collected and processed.
  • Object to certain processing, including processing based on consent or direct marketing where applicable.
  • Access personal data we hold about you and information about how it is processed.
  • Correct inaccurate, outdated, incomplete, or misleading personal data.
  • Erase or block personal data in circumstances allowed by law.
  • Data portability for covered data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format, where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • File a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority, including the National Privacy Commission of the Philippines where applicable.

To exercise a right, please contact us using the details below. We may request additional information to verify your identity, understand your request, and protect the privacy of others.

Minors

Children's privacy

Our general corporate website and business services are not directed to children. Some client-operated ticketing, venue, event, education, or family entertainment services may involve minors. Where we process children's personal data, we do so only when necessary, lawful, proportionate, and subject to applicable consent, authorization, and safeguarding requirements.

External sites

Third-party websites and services

Our website, emails, or software may link to third-party websites, payment gateways, social media pages, client portals, or partner services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. Please review their privacy notices before submitting personal data to them.

Updates

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, practices, technologies, legal requirements, or business operations. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Material changes may be communicated through additional notices where appropriate.
